ON-CALL EMPLOYEE AGREEMENT (W-2 | At-Will)

This Agreement is entered into by and between Well Done Construction LLC and the undersigned Employee.

1. EMPLOYMENT STATUS

Employee is hired as a W-2, at-will, on-call employee. No minimum hours are guaranteed. Work is offered on an as-needed basis and may be accepted or declined by the Employee. Employment may be terminated by either party at any time, with or without cause or notice, in accordance with applicable law.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

Employee may be assigned duties including general carpentry and construction labor, repairs, site documentation, and photographs. Employee may NOT provide pricing, modify the scope of work without authorization, or pull permits.

3. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS

Employee is expected to perform all work in a professional and competent manner. Failure to correct substandard work or unsafe practices are grounds for immediate termination.

8. SAFETY & OSHA COMPLIANCE

Employee must comply with all job site safety rules. OSHA-10 certification is required. Proper personal protective equipment (PPE) must be used at all times.

10. CONFIDENTIALITY & PROTECTION

Employee may not solicit Company clients for personal work or use Company contacts for personal gain. These obligations survive termination of employment.
